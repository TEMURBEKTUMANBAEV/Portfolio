Tips for everyone for life

"Success does not consist in never making mistakes but in never making the same one a second time."

1. Take time to get to know yourself.

"Know thyself," said Aristotle. When you know who you are, you can choose the right goals and have authentic beliefs. Knowing yourself means living with purpose and meaning.

2.Live in the present.

Don't nostalgize for the past, don't dream about the future, but concentrate on being fully here and now-this is the only place and time where you can make a difference with your actions.

3.Be patient and persistent.

In life, it's not so important what you've achieved - it's more important what you've had to overcome.

4.Luck comes to those who work hard.

Luck happens when your diligence and talent have waited for their finest hour.
